Siddagangashree Voluntary Blood Center

Siddagangashree Voluntary Blood Center is a dedicated group that acts as a bridge between donors and hospitals. Our initiative, the Social Connect Group, was started a few years ago with the aim of ensuring a steady and timely supply of blood to those in need.

Our Process

We collect blood from self-interested donors whenever possible and store it for emergencies. Hospitals can access this blood when required, and individuals can also obtain blood from our organization when necessary.

Precautionary Measures

    -All equipment is sterilized to ensure a safe donation process.
    -Donors are screened for eligibility and health conditions.
    -Trained professionals handle the blood collection process.
    -After donation, donors are provided with refreshments and rest.
