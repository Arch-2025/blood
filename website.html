MAJOR GROUPS

O,A,AB,B

DONOR CONDITIONS

  • Age between 18 and 60 years
  • Haemoglobin - not less than 12.5 g/Dl
  • Pulse - between 50 and 100/minute with no irregularities
  • Blood Pressure -Systolic 100-180 mm Hg

USES

  • help your blood flow and result in fewer arterial blockages
  • reduce their risk of having a heart attack or stroke
  • Donating can keep your iron level balanced
